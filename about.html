Nishi Services – Complete Hospitality Solution

Nishi Services’ mission is to achieve exponential growth with all our clients by forming strategic partnerships that allow us to build new hotels and work with under-performing hotels to position them for growth advancement. We aim to be leaders in the field of hospitality management with a proven track record of success.

With extensive experience of providing hospitality consulting services, we truly understand various hotel development stages and their specific requirements.

Whether you are building a new hotel property or re-branding an existing hotel, at Nishi Services, we can guide you to make an informed choice on your brand selection, project management and hotel commissioning.

For operational assets, we can review the existing operations and suggest new ways to optimize costs and drive operational efficiencies and improve the top-line revenues thereby contributing to commercial success of your hotel investment.

Our hospitality solutions are tailored to your needs and we shall be happy to support hotel asset owners.
